## 📝 PREP NOTES

**Sunday Batch Cooking:**
- Cook grains for the week (quinoa, farro, brown rice)
- Prep minestrone soup (Sunday lunch + extras)
- Roast vegetables in bulk (carrots, Brussels sprouts)
- Hard-boil eggs for quick breakfasts

**Storage Tips:**
- Keep fermented foods refrigerated
- Store greens in airtight containers with paper towels
- Portion out chia/flax into small containers for easy morning use

**Cost-Saving Tips:**
- Buy dried beans/lentils instead of canned (cheaper, more nutritious)
- Frozen berries work great for smoothies and oats
- Canned sardines/salmon are budget-friendly omega-3 sources
- Buy carrots, celery, onions in bulk (used throughout the week)
