## 📝 PREP NOTES

**Sunday Batch Cooking (~45 min):**
- Cook quinoa for the week (makes 4-5 cups)
- Cook barley (makes 1 cup)
- Roast sweet potatoes + broccoli in bulk
- Hard-boil 4-6 eggs for quick breakfasts
- Prep white bean soup base (Sunday lunch + extras)

**Make-Ahead Tips:**
- Overnight oats: prep Monday night for Tuesday breakfast
- **Oatmeal is 5 min** — don't skip it, just cook it fresh
- Sardine/tuna salads take 3 min — no excuse
- Chicken breast: 12 min in a hot pan, rest 5 min

**Storage Tips:**
- Store greens with paper towels in airtight containers
- Portion chia/flax into small containers for easy morning use
- Keep cut veggies in water in the fridge

**Cost-Saving Tips:**
- Canned fish is budget-friendly omega-3
- Frozen berries work great for oatmeal and smoothies
- Buy carrots, onions, garlic in bulk (used throughout the week)
- Dried beans are cheaper than canned (but prep time is higher)
